About

I am an interdisciplinary fine artist hailing from Dallas, TX. Using a range of painting mediums, I aim to create a world of unexpected juxtapositions.

All life exists on a fixed timeline. Nature’s impatience when reclaiming deserted structures and landscapes serve as a constant reminder of the perpetual passing of time.

My paintings aim to pinpoint this process by composing wide-reaching landscapes occupied by isolated animals and organisms. The intertwining of natural elements with decay creates a story of demise and rebirth.

Flamingo Falls

24"X36" Acrylic on Canvas

Flamingo Falls is inspired by the famous lava flows Hawaii. The power and natural beauty of these destructive rivers of magma set the backdrop of my newest addition to my Birds of Paradise Series.

The stark contrast of the lava stream against the ingenious cliff face paired nicely with the signature pink feathers of our winged friend the flamingo. Together in harmony the two meet at sea neither belonging nor detracting from the coastal beauty of the big island.

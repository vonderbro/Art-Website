Bullfinch at Dusk

14” X 11” Oil on Canvas

Bullfinch at Dusk combines the charming bullfinch with the captivating prickly pear. On a hike through Caprock Canyon, the setting sun cast a technicolor glow across the canyon walls and endless throngs of prickly pear shrubs.

Native to Europe and Asia, the unlikely bullfinch was chosen for its complementary coloring. Despite departing from the industrial motif, the painting served as a pivotal piece from acrylic to oil as a medium.

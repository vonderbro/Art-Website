Land Unfulfilled

24” X 36” Acrylic on Canvas

The first painting in the animal series, Land Unfulfilled was the introduction to the theme of animals juxtaposed against industrial environments.

The rolling backdrop of mountainous garbage was originally inspired by movie scenes from Wall-E and Idiocracy. Reminiscent of the composition of Flowers for Astronaut, the heavy usage of atmospheric perspective and carefully placed bulldozer is meant to pull the viewer in.
